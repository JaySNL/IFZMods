## How to use this page

- **Players** — found a bug or have a request? [Open an issue](https://github.com/JaySNL/IFZMods/issues/new/choose). Include your save day, OS (Windows / Proton / Mac), and which mods are installed.
- **Dev** — when a bug is reported, add a row to *Open bugs*; move to 🟢 Fixed (with the version) once shipped, then prune after a couple of cycles.
- Release-pipeline detail (BUILT → VERIFIED → PUSHED) lives in `tools/nexus-publish/RELEASE-QUEUE.md`; this page is the public-facing summary.
